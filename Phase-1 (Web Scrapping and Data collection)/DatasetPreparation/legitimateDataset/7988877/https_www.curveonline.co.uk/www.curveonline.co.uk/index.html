Initial casting announced for Jesus Christ Superstar

We're excited to welcome some familiar faces back to Curve when the award-winning, reimagined production of Tim Rice and Andrew Lloyd Webber's celebrated musical comes to Curve in February 2024!

Olivier Award-nominee Ian McIntosh - who played Sid Worley in our 2018 Made at Curve production of An Officer and a Gentleman the Musical - will play Jesus, Shem Omari James, who recently appeared in the UK tour of Dreamgirls and helped us welcome audiences back in our 2021 Made at Curve production of The Music of Andrew Lloyd Webber, will play Judas, and Hannah Richardson (Sting's The Last Ship) will play Mary.

Kali Discovery Programme now open!

Are you an aspiring South Asian female writer? Have you ever thought about writing a play?

Kali Theatre has launched its DISCOVERY Programme to search for talented new South Asian women playwrights across three UK cities.

DISCOVERY is a free writing programme for early stage female writers in Birmingham, Leicester and London. Working with partners Curve, Birmingham Repertory Theatre, and London’s Theatre503, Kali invites women of all ages and all South Asian backgrounds to apply.

Connecting Cultures: A Moving Together Showcase

Thu 29 Jun

Come and experience Moving Together’s TMET Dance Festival 2023, showcasing Primary and Secondary school groups from across The Mead Educational Trust who will present their explorations into this year’s theme of Culture.
Moving Together is a creative community dance company based in Leicester, providing dance and development opportunities for children, young people, aspiring practitioners and adults over the age of 55 within Leicestershire and the surrounding counties.

Matthew Bourne's Romeo and Juliet

Mon 3 Jul — Sat 8 Jul

Matthew Bourne’s Romeo and Juliet gives Shakespeare’s timeless story of forbidden love a scintillating injection of raw passion and youthful vitality.

A masterful re-telling of an ageless tale of teenage discovery and the madness of first love, Romeo and Juliet garnered universal critical acclaim when it premiered in 2019, and now returns to the New Adventures repertoire alongside the very best of Bourne’s world renowned dance theatre productions.

Wish You Were Dead

Tue 18 Jul — Sat 22 Jul

Following on from five hit stage shows and the new acclaimed ITV series, Grace, the work of best selling author, Peter James, returns to Curve with the world premiere stage adaption of Wish You Were Dead. An all star cast includes Katie McGlynn, best known for her award-winning time on Coronation Street, George Rainsford, who returns to the stage after eight years as Ethan Hardy in BBC One’s Casualty and Clive Mantle, much loved for many roles including Casualty, Game of Thrones and the iconic ‘puddle’ scene in Vicar of Dibley.

Learn with top artists in Curve Classroom

Since January, we're proud to have supported home-schooling during the third national lockdown with our educational initiative, Curve Classroom.

The free sessions cover a range of topics for all ages and are delivered by a host of leading artists including performers Molly Lynch, Garry Robson and Hareet Deol, Designers Grace Smart and Kate Unwin, Director Julia Thomas, Choreographers Arlene Phillips and Mel Knott, Writers Jess Green and Rob Ward, as well as a number of Midlands-based artists local to Curve in Leicester.
