AT&T launches 2Gbps, 5Gbps fibre in 70 metro areas

25 Jan 2022

AT&T Communications has introduced multi-gigabit transmission speeds for its fibre-to-the-home (FTTH) customers. Nearly 5.2 million customer locations in parts of more than 70 metro areas – including Los Angeles, Atlanta and Dallas – will be able to take advantage of symmetrical 2Gbps and 5Gbps speed tiers. The 2Gbps package is priced at USD110 per month, while the 5Gbps service costs USD180 per month (tariffs exclude taxes).

The telco says it will continue to introduce multi-gig-capable technology across its existing fibre footprint throughout 2022 and seeks to cover 30 million customer locations by year-end 2025.
