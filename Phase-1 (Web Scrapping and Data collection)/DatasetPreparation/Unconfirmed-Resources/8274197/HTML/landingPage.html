Welcome to BexCabs Norfolk

BexCabs Norfolk is a locally based taxi service operating
in the Fakenham and surrounding area.
We can provide taxis for nights out, appointments, shopping, hospitals, airports & train stations, festivals, special events, racecourses, functions and nationwide destinations.
We are licensed by North Norfolk District Council
​and hold current DBS certificates with first aid training
& excellent customer service skills.
