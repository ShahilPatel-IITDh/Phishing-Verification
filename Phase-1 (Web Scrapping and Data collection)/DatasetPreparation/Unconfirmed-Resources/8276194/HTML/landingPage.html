About De Grisogono Tower

De Grisogono Tower offers 1, 2, and 3 bedroom apartments along with additional maid rooms. The project features two towers — Tower-A and Tower-B. Tower-A includes 61 floors, and all apartments after the 41st floor are duplexes to ensure you get all the comfort and space. From this tower, you get mesmerizing island and sea views that are tough to miss out on. The Tower-B has 45 floors with apartments that have a charming view of the Burj Al Arab, Palm, and Atlantis hotel. Both the towers are connected by an enchanted hanging garden, an added feature that elevates your living experience. With the highest quality materials and top-notch interior designs, Damac has gone above and beyond so you can experience the nature of luxury right in the heart of the city.
