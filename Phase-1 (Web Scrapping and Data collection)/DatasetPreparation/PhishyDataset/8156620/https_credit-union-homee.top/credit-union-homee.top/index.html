Welcome to Park City, Utah

A world-renowned destination for outdoor adventure and mountain town charm. Nestled in the heart of the breathtaking Wasatch Mountains, Park City offers year-round recreation, stunning scenery, and a vibrant cultural scene. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or a longer stay, you'll discover endless opportunities for outdoor activities such as skiing, snowboarding, hiking, mountain biking, fishing, and golfing.

The best part is that Park City is dedicated to sustainable tourism and taking steps to be a leader in responsible travel. We invite you to visit with care, respecting the environment and the local community. Our town is also conveniently located just 35 minutes away from Salt Lake City International Airport, providing easy access to your mountain retreat. Immerse yourself in the festivals and events that showcase the best in food, wine, art, and music, and explore our historic Main Street, lined with charming shops, delightful restaurants, and cozy cafes. Park City truly has something for everyone, so get ready to experience all the wonders this remarkable destination has to offer. We can't wait to welcome you to Park City, Utah!
